ABOUT US



This community was created for tourists, residents, you, and me, to learn more about the beautiful city of Valencia in Spain. There are a lot of stories to tell; the culture; the food; the people; the football clubs; fast cars; etc.

This is a community for story tellers and story readers. If you’ve been to Valencia, there’s something you must have seen that none of us has or didn't pay attention to. If you haven't been here, then there's a lot to learn from the experiences of others that have been here.

This is home to all, irrespective of race, colour, culture or origin. Please respect community guidelines an show basic empathy to everyone.

For inquiries, please send a mail to g.akande@rgu.ac.uk. We’d do our best to respond in 48 hours. As you were!!! Read a story.
